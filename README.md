# 🏯 The Casbah's Daughter
### *A Story of Courage, Family, and the Secrets of Algiers*

> **3D Action-Adventure | Unity | PC | 2025–2026**

---

## 📖 About the Game

**The Casbah's Daughter** is a story-driven 3D action-adventure game set in the heart of **medieval Algiers**. You play as a brave young woman whose father — an elderly geologist who holds the secret location of a great buried treasure — is kidnapped by Spanish pirates. Armed with wit, courage, and her fists, she must follow the pirates through the winding streets of the Casbah, infiltrate their ship at the harbor, uncover dark secrets, and confront a fearsome pirate captain to bring her father home.

The game celebrates the **history, architecture, and culture of Algiers**, immersing players in the sights and sounds of the legendary Casbah — the labyrinthine old city that forms the heart of the game's identity.

## 🗺️ Story

### Background
In the heart of the Qasbah of Algiers, a young woman lives peacefully with her father — an old geologist who holds a dangerous secret: the location of a great treasure hidden somewhere within the Casbah. Spanish pirates, hungry for this knowledge, raid their home one fateful night. She is knocked unconscious. When she awakens, her father is gone.

### Inciting Incident
A year passes with no news. Then, one day in the marketplace, she spots a pirate she recognizes from that terrible night. Fueled by hope and vengeance, she decides to follow him — and so the journey begins.

### Resolution
**Victory:** She rescues her father. The ship explodes and begins to sink. They escape together in a triumphant, emotional ending.

**Defeat:** If the protagonist is killed by the final boss or captured by guards, the scene replays from the last checkpoint.

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Characters

### Protagonist
A 21-year-old brave, clever, trained fighter. Her goal is simple: rescue her father at any cost.

### The Father
An elderly geologist with deep knowledge of the Algiers Casbah. The pirates believe he knows the location of a great buried treasure. Saving him is the player's ultimate goal.

### Enemies — Simple Pirates
Spanish pirates serving under the captain. They patrol the Casbah and the ship and will give chase if they detect the protagonist. They attack with melee strikes.

### Final Boss — The Pirate Captain
The game's primary villain. Unmistakable: a large belly, a metal leg, and an eye patch. Accompanied by his loyal parrot. His attacks are powerful, he has significantly more health than ordinary pirates, and he has unique attack patterns that require the player to adapt their strategy.

### NPCs
NPCs populate the Casbah and provide crucial information. Each NPC has a maximum of 3 dialogue interactions. Key figures include market vendors, an informant who knows the ship's location, and prisoners aboard the ship who hold clues.

## 🎯 Gameplay

### Core Loop
```
Explore → Gather Information → Infiltrate → Solve → Combat → Progress
```

### Health System
The protagonist has a health bar displayed in the HUD. Medicine items are found throughout the environment and stored in the inventory. The player uses them manually to restore health.

### Stealth & Infiltration
During the ship infiltration sequence, the protagonist must avoid detection by patrolling guards. Guards have a detection range and a field of view. The player can distract guards to create safe passage. Getting caught triggers a defeat and replays the scene.

### Combat
Melee-based combat. The player initiates attacks; enemies have visible health bars and react to damage. The final boss has unique patterns that demand strategic adaptation.

## 📋 Quest Structure

| Quest | Description |
|---|---|
| **Opening Cinematic** | Father is kidnapped by Spanish pirates. Protagonist is knocked unconscious. Stakes are established. |
| **Quest 1 — Follow the Pirate** | Protagonist spawns in the Casbah, spots a pirate from that night, and follows him through the streets until he disappears. |
| **Quest 2 — Gather Information** | Ask Casbah residents for clues. An informant reveals the ship's location. Climb a tall building and use binoculars to spot the pirate boarding his ship at the harbor. |
| **Quest 3 — Infiltrate the Ship** | Travel to the harbor. Distract guards and sneak aboard the pirate ship without being detected. |
| **Quest 4 — Explore the Ship** | Navigate the ship's decks, cabins, and cargo holds while avoiding patrolling pirates. |
| **Quest 5 — Find the Prison** | Search for clues aboard the ship that reveal where prisoners are held. Locate the cell where the father is kept. |
| **Quest 6 — Final Boss Fight** | Confront the pirate captain. Survive his powerful attacks and defeat him. |
| **Quest 7 — Rescue & Escape** | Rescue the father. Explosions break out on the sinking ship. Escape together in the ending cutscene. |

## 🌍 Game World & Environments

### The Casbah of Algiers
A labyrinthine network of narrow alleys, white-washed buildings, arched doorways, tiled fountains, and bustling marketplaces. The Casbah is the heart of the game's identity and celebrates Algerian history and culture. All 3D assets were created or adapted in **Blender**.

### The Harbor & Pirate Ship
The harbor connects the Casbah to the sea. A large wooden pirate vessel is anchored here. The ship interior includes multiple decks, a captain's cabin, cargo holds, and a prison cell area — with ropes, barrels, lanterns, and cannons throughout.

### Atmosphere
- Daytime and dusk lighting over the Casbah
- Ocean atmosphere at the harbor: waves, salt air, sea sounds
- Dimly lit, oppressive interior of the pirate ship

## ⚙️ Unity Technical Systems

### Player Control System
Third-person perspective. Walk, run, crouch, jump. Camera rotates around the player.
**Unity Components:** `CharacterController`, Cinemachine camera

### Collision System
Prevents the player from passing through walls, ship structures, and doors. Trigger zones activate dialogue, quest events, and restricted area detection.
**Unity Components:** `BoxCollider`, `CapsuleCollider`, Trigger Colliders

### Dialogue System
Triggered when the player enters an NPC's interaction zone and presses the interaction key. A dialogue box appears with NPC text and player response options. Key dialogues unlock quest progression. Max 3 interactions per NPC.

### Quest System
Tracks and manages all 7 main quests. The active quest objective is always displayed in the HUD. Internally manages story flags and scene transitions.

### Enemy AI System
Pirates patrol designated areas, detect the player within a set range and field of view, give chase, and attack with melee strikes.
**Unity Components:** `NavMeshAgent`, custom AI state machine (Patrol → Detect → Chase → Attack)

### Combat System
Attack animations triggered by player input. Enemy health system with hitbox/trigger detection. The final boss has enhanced health, stronger attacks, and unique patterns.
**Unity Components:** `Animator`, Collider-based hitboxes

### Scene & Level Management
Three major scene categories:
- Casbah (opening, investigation)
- Harbor & Ship Exterior (infiltration)
- Ship Interior (exploration, boss fight, escape)

Transitions managed with Unity's `SceneManager`. Load screens or cutscenes bridge major transitions.

### UI System
**Unity Component:** Canvas UI System (Screen Space Overlay)

### Audio System
Music tracks triggered per zone and game state. Spatial sound effects for environmental immersion.
**Unity Components:** `AudioSource`, `AudioListener` (on camera), `AudioMixer`

## 🚀 How to Open the Project

1. Install **Unity Hub** and the appropriate Unity version (check `ProjectSettings/ProjectVersion.txt` for the exact version).
2. Clone or extract the project folder.
3. Open **Unity Hub** → click **Add** → select the `Projet-CJRV/` folder.
4. Open the project. Unity will import all assets automatically.
5. Open the main scene from `Assets/Scenes/` and press **Play** to run the game.

> Make sure all Blender-exported `.fbx` or `.obj` files are present in the `Assets/` folder before opening. Unity will reference them automatically.
